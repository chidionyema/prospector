Regulatory shock in UK haulage sector: HMRC red-diesel reform (April 2022) removed entitlement to fuel-duty relief ("PTO rebate") for hauliers. Previously, off-road diesel was 11p/litre cheaper than on-road. The change eliminates a major variable cost advantage for the sector, affects thousands of small operators, and creates acute pain around compliance and cost recovery.
