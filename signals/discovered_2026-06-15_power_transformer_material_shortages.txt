Global supply chain constraints on grain-oriented electrical steel have extended lead times for critical power transformers to multiple years, creating a bottleneck that halts renewable energy interconnection and stalls infrastructure resilience. Utilities and wind/solar developers are left managing asset failures with aging stock, facing astronomical opportunity costs for every day a project remains off-grid. This creates an opening for specialized asset-management platforms that optimize transformer life-extension, predictive maintenance, and localized supply-sharing networks to mitigate the physical shortage.
