The FuelEU Maritime regulation introduces GHG intensity limits on shipping fuel, with penalties that compound exponentially for each year of non-compliance. Ship operators, particularly those managing older or lower-efficiency fleets, are entering a phase where the cost of the penalty will quickly exceed the margin of the shipping route itself. This creates an adversarial demand for real-time fleet route optimization and fuel-switching strategy tools that can dynamically hedge against these compounding penalties, moving beyond simple compliance filing to aggressive cost-avoidance.
