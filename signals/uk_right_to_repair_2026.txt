Signal: The UK's expanded Right to Repair regulations (in force 2025-2026) now
require manufacturers of more appliance and electronics categories to supply
spare parts and repair information to independent repairers for up to 10 years.
Independent repair shops complain they still cannot get affordable, structured
access to OEM parts catalogues, diagnostic codes, and repair manuals — each
brand has a different portal, pricing, and data format. Consumers report long
waits and refusals. Trading Standards is starting to act on non-compliance.
