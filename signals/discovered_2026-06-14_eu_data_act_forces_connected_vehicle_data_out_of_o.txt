The EU Data Act (Regulation 2023/2854) entered application on 12 September 2025, giving users of connected products — vehicles included — the right to access the data their cars generate and redirect it to third parties of their choice. OEMs, who have monetised exclusive telematics access for years, must now expose this stream to independent repairers, usage-based insurers and fleet operators, and they are resisting via technical friction, throttled interfaces and restrictive terms. The independent aftermarket and UBI players are newly enabled but cannot realise the value without help extracting, normalising and enforcing access against obstructive manufacturers — a liberation tool no OEM will ever build against its own book. Why now: the application date has just passed and the first concrete access disputes are surfacing.
