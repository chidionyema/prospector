Lead times for large power and distribution transformers have blown out to 2-4 years with prices up 3-4x since 2022, as a handful of OEMs cannot scale grain-oriented electrical steel and winding capacity fast enough for the electrification and data-centre boom. Developers, utilities and hyperscalers now sit on stranded interconnection slots and idle capital because they cannot get iron, and the OEMs who are the bottleneck have no incentive to ship the cure. Value accrues to whoever can broker, refurbish, re-spec, queue-optimise or de-risk transformer procurement, and it scales as scarcity worsens.
