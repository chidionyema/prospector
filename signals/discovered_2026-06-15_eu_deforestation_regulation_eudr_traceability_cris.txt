The EUDR requires importers to provide geolocated traceability for products like beef, soy, and timber, imposing heavy penalties for non-compliance. Small and medium-sized enterprises lack the technical infrastructure to map complex, multi-tier supply chains to the plot-of-land level. As enforcement ramps up and audits become aggressive, the pain for these companies grows, creating a massive opening for robust, automated supply-chain verification and forensic data tools that the regulator itself cannot provide.
