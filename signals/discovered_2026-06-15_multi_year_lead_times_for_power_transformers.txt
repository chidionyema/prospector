A fundamental supply-chain dislocation has emerged due to a global surge in demand for power transformers from data centers, EV infrastructure, and renewable energy grid integration. Utilities and developers are facing lead times of 3 to 5 years, stalling critical projects and risking grid reliability. The bottleneck is physical—raw material shortages and capacity constraints—making this a severe pain point where the value of securing supply or optimizing existing hardware performance grows daily.
